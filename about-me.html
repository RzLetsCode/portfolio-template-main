How it all started...

I did not start with a perfect roadmap into AI and programming. I started with curiosity, a second-hand laptop, and the feeling that software could change the way people solve problems in the real world. Writing my first few lines of code and seeing them actually work was enough to hook me for good.

- From tinkering with basic scripts to designing enterprise-grade AI platforms.

Over the years, that curiosity turned into a career where I now work as an Enterprise AI Architect and Senior Data Scientist, designing data platforms, ML systems, and GenAI solutions that real teams use every day. I still approach new technologies the same way I did on day one: experiment, break things, learn fast, and then turn those lessons into reliable, scalable systems others can build on.
