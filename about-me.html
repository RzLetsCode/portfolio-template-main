Who Am I?

I am Rajesh Kumar, an Enterprise AI Architect who loves turning complex data and business problems into pragmatic AI solutions that actually ship to production.

I design and build end‑to‑end systems across data engineering, machine learning, and generative AI—using Python, PySpark, Databricks, Azure, and modern MLOps practices—to create reliable, scalable, and cost‑efficient AI platforms for enterprises.

Beyond hands‑on architecture and model development, I enjoy documenting what I learn, mentoring teams, and sharing ideas through my portfolio and blog so that others can build better data and AI systems faster.

This is important build AI that is useful, reliable, and simple enough for real teams to adopt.

How it all started...

I worked with enterprise requirements and as the world adopted AI, our company did too, which pushed me to evolve from Data Scientist to AI Architect with a clear roadmap into AI and programming.

- From tinkering with basic scripts to designing enterprise-grade AI platforms.

I now work as an Enterprise AI Architect, designing GenAI solutions that real teams use every day. I still approach new technologies the same way I did on day one: experiment, break things, learn fast, and then turn those lessons into reliable, scalable systems others can build on.

Future plans
