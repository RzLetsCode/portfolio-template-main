Start Your AI Journey

Follow these steps to transform your career with AI and Data Science

1

Assess Your Current Skills

Understand where you stand in your AI/Data Science journey. Identify your strengths and areas for improvement.

  • Evaluate your programming knowledge (Python, SQL)
  • Assess your mathematical foundation (Statistics, Linear Algebra)
  • Review your domain knowledge and experience
2

Choose Your Learning Path

Select the track that aligns with your career goals and current experience level.

Beginner

Start with Python basics, data structures, and foundational ML concepts

Intermediate

Deep dive into ML algorithms, data pipelines, and cloud platforms

Advanced

Master LLMs, RAG systems, agentic AI, and enterprise architecture

3

Build Real-World Projects

Apply your knowledge by building portfolio projects that showcase your skills to employers.

  • Create end-to-end ML pipelines with real datasets
  • Build chatbots using LangChain and RAG architecture
  • Deploy models on cloud platforms (Azure, AWS, GCP)
  • Maintain clean GitHub repos with proper documentation
4

Network and Get Mentorship

Connect with industry professionals and get guidance from experienced mentors.

  • Join AI/ML communities and attend meetups
  • Build your LinkedIn presence and share insights
  • Participate in discussions on AI forums and Discord
5

Land Your Dream Job

Prepare for interviews and apply strategically to AI/Data Science roles.

  • Optimize your resume for ATS systems
  • Practice coding problems and system design
  • Target companies aligned with your goals
